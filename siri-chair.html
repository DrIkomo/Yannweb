Siri Chair

I build a playful interactive chair to understand the basics of human-computer interaction. From the hardware and prototyping of the whole system to the interactive design and software required to do a chair can play with people. It may make some voice when you sit down on them. I used Openframeworks and flex-force sensor to do Siri Chair detection and transfer the data and  sound.

context: concept design, Openframeworks, Arduino, sensors.
