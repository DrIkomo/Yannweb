Smiley

Smiley is INTERACTIVE SCREEN INSTALLATION. I investigated the issue of technology and human facial expression and their behaviors. It detects human facial expressions when people feel happy or sad. Their moods will change the poster on the screen.

context: concept design, processing coding, FaceOSC, graphic design, motion design

I used adobe aftereffects to make screen content and Processing to create a face-detection program. When audiences approach to screen with normal picture, the Processing program(sketch) detects their faces from a camera on the top of the screen. And the program recognize people's faces and change the normal picture to happiness or sadness one.
