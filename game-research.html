Game Research

This part is all about my indie game design. I create some indie games. Some of them based on storytelling and others are based on game engine technique and gamplay. I will keep try more kinds of different indie games.

context: game design, programing, character design, graphic design, motion design, UX

# .01

Last Circle

The game is based on people's short-term memory. The last time you tap the last generative circle on the screen the more score you win.

 

with: Shudao Wang

role: interactive design,and gameplay

# .02

Creepy Doll

Once, there were many toy dolls want to escape from scary park. You could use some tools to help the dolls. Choosing and drag the useful objects to help the dolls in different levels.

with: Yuqing Zen

role: gameplay, level design,program, unity, art design,GUI, animation

# .03

Capsule Factory

Capsule factory is a student work of mine, I use unity in order to develop a physical simulation game.

Players can utilize boxes to avoid the obstacles three times.

context: unity, photoshop

# .04

LU HUA

The game concept comes from people who feel sick when they are sitting back of cars. Players use direction and space to avoid stranger are sitting behind him.

context: unity, maya, photoshop

# .05

Ming' s Dream

I use this game to  tell a warm story about my strange dream.

context: storytelling, unity, level design.

# .06

Funny Lab

Funny lab is a puzzle game with three direction. Player need find three up squares with same colors. The squares arrays magic cube. Player need change different direction to find the squares.

context: 3D,unity, puzzle game
