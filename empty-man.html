Empty Man

This is a story about a boy who finds his heart. In a strange city, every one are robots, they are working in a smoky factory, they have produced smoke to the air one by one. A little boy with a empty heart can not produce smoke, so the leader of factory have thrown out boy. Little boy need find his heart.
